+ Skins/Skinning
  • Every skin uses only one image for the actual skin and two for the shape of the form.
  • Open the "main.jpg" in your image editor and use that to design your own personal skin.
  • After you are finished make a new directory in the "skins" directory and label it as the name of your skin, and save your new skin into it as "main.jpg" it MUST be all lowercase."
  • Then make a new image 262x133 pixels, design the shape of your maximized form using black as the form and white as the transparent area, and leave a white 1 pixel border around the image. Now save the image in your skin directory as "shapemax.jpg" again in all lowercase.
  • Now make another new image 262x18 pixels ang use black for the shape and white as the transparent area, this will be your minimized form. Again leave a white 1 pixel border around the edge, and save it as "shapemin.jpg" in lowercase letters.
  • Next find the "colors.txt" file in the default skin directory and edit the "Foreground/Background/Time" colors using RGB format, 0-255. In the "About" section fill in information about yor skin or leave your website url for extra recognition :)
  • Now your skin is ready!
